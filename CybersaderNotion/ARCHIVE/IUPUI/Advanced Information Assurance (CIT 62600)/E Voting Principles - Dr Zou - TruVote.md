# Transparent, Verifiable, Assurable, and Remote Electronic Voting System for Open and Fair Elections

- interest conflicting parties that won’t share info or collude act of tallying authorities
- protected against quantum computing
- Voter gets secret location
    - voter doesn’t know other voters’ location or which votes go to each location
- Doesn’t scale well because of random number generation with the secret location anonymization
    - Solution: divide large populations into distributed voting groups
